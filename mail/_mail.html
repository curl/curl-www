Etiquette

Mailing lists is the preferred method of communication with and within the curl project. If you have a questions on how to do things, if things do not work out the way you expect them to or if you want to contribute patches or offer other help.

All curl-related lists require that you are subscribed before you are allowed to post to them. All posts to these lists are archived in public archives on the web.
